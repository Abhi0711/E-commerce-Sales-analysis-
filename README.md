# E-commerce-Sales-analysis-
Business Problem: An e-commerce company wants to understand sales patterns, customer behavior, and product performance to increase revenue and optimize marketing spend.
Sales Performance:
What is the overall revenue trend? (Monthly/Quarterly)
Which months have highest/lowest sales?
What's the average order value (AOV)?
What's the sales growth rate?

Customer Analysis:
How many unique customers do we have?
What's the customer retention rate?
Who are our top customers? (RFM Analysis)
What's the customer lifetime value (CLV)?
What does the customer cohort analysis show?

Product Analysis:
What are the top 10 best-selling products?
Which products have highest revenue?
Which products are underperforming?
What's the product return rate?

Geographic Analysis:
Which countries/regions generate most revenue?
Where should we focus marketing efforts?

Business Recommendations:
Which customer segments should we target?
What products should we promote?
When should we run campaigns?
